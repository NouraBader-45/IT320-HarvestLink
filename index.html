Reducing agricultural waste through clear digital coordination.

HarvestLink is a web-based platform that connects farmers with surplus agricultural products to charitable organizations in need. The system supports listing products, submitting requests, responding to requests, and monitoring core activities in a clear and simple way.

About HarvestLink

Built to support transparent, organized, and role-based coordination.

For Farmers

Farmers can post surplus agricultural products, manage listings, and review incoming donation requests.

For Charities

Charitable organizations can browse available products, view details, submit requests, and track request status.

For Administrators

Administrators can monitor system activity, manage listings, and block inappropriate users or products when needed.

How It Works

A simple process focused on digital coordination only.

1. Farmers List Products

Farmers add surplus product information such as crop type, quantity, expiration date, and condition.

2. Charities Browse and Request

Organizations search available products, view details, and submit requests for needed items.

3. Farmers Review Requests

Incoming requests can be viewed and responded to through approval or rejection.

4. Admin Monitors the Platform

Administrators review activity, block inappropriate accounts, and maintain overall platform reliability.
