About HarvestLink

Built to support transparent, organized, and role-based coordination.

For Farmers

Farmers can post surplus products, manage listings, and review incoming donation requests.

For Charities

Charities can browse available products, view details, submit requests, and track status.

For Administrators

Administrators can monitor activity, manage users, and manage listings.

How It Works

A simple process focused on digital coordination only.

1. Farmers List Products

Farmers add crop type, quantity, expiration date, and condition.

2. Charities Browse and Request

Organizations search available products, view details, and submit requests.

3. Farmers Review Requests

Incoming requests can be approved or rejected.

4. Admin Monitors the Platform

Administrators keep the platform organized and reliable.
